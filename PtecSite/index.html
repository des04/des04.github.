School is different around here...

PTEC stands for Pembina Trails Early College and is an advanced technologically focused education program for highschool students within the Pembina Trails School Division. Sudents within the program learn tech skills that can be applied in the workplace. Students over two years take courses in Software Development, IT, and Networking, all on top of regular mandatory school classes such as math, science, and ELA.
